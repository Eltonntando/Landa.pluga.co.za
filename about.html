About Me

I am Yolanda Shabangu, well known as Poetess Landa "Libhubesikati" meaning "The lioness". I was born in the dusty streets of an unrecognizable village, namely Magogeni, in the Mpumalanga province of South Africa. I am a siSwati poet and a multiple brand ambassador. I am looking forward to inspiring you with my poetry, heal you, mend your broken hearts and give you hope, as well as educate through poetry. Let me take you through my journey,welcome! For bookings call/e-mail me.
Yolanda Shabangu, born on the 24th of August, 2002, known to many as Poetess Landa, holds the title "Libhubesikati," meaning "The Lioness." Her story begins amidst the humble and unassuming streets of Magogeni, an uncelebrated village nestled in the heart of Mpumalanga province in South Africa.
From a young age, Yolanda displayed a profound passion for public speaking, a flame that was kindled during her school years. The power of expression through words captivated her soul, becoming the hallmark of her identity. Writing became her trusted tool, her sanctuary, a means to mend her broken heart following the loss of her mother in the year 2016.Yolanda's grief led her to a transformational path - the birth of a poet. Through her verses, she found healing, and in her words, she discovered her purpose. Her poetic journey gained momentum, and she emerged as a SiSwati poet, using her native language as the canvas for her craft.
In 2022, her dedication and talent were recognized, earning her the prestigious title of "Hustling Honeys Best Poet of the Year." This accolade marked a significant milestone in her career, inspiring her to reach even greater heights. Yolanda's influence extended beyond the written word. She became a beacon in the media landscape, gracing the airwaves through multiple radio interviews with Mpumalanga radio stations. Through these platforms, she amplified her poetic voice, captivating audiences and spreading the essence of her message.
Yet, her aspirations reach beyond personal achievements. Yolanda envisions a world where her poetry becomes a beacon of hope and healing, touching and mending the hearts of those who bear the burden of brokenness. Her ultimate goal is to inspire the youth and upcoming poets, guiding them to unleash their potential, find their voices, and embrace the transformative power of words. With a heart dedicated to the art of poetry and a spirit yearning to illuminate the world, Yolanda Shabangu, "Libhubesikati," continues her journey as a lioness of words, sharing her passion and purpose with all who seek inspiration.
